## Where Did This Meme Come From?

The meme is a combination of two unrelated images joined together around 2019.

### The Woman: Taylor Armstrong

The left panel is a screenshot from **The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills**, specifically from a Season 2 reunion episode. The woman is **Taylor Armstrong**, and she is crying and pointing at co-star Kyle Richards during a heated argument.

The clip became a meme on its own for a while — an intense, dramatic reaction that could be applied to anything.

### The Cat: Smudge

The right panel features **Smudge**, a cat owned by Miranda Weingartner. The photo — Smudge sitting behind a dinner table in front of a salad, looking deeply unimpressed — was posted to Tumblr in 2018.

The photo exists because cats occasionally do sit at dinner tables when placed there. The specific expression Smudge has — dignified, slightly contemptuous, absolutely not moving — is what makes the image so usable.

### The Combination

Someone on Twitter combined the two images in May 2019. The format exploded within days, becoming one of the most shared meme formats of that year.

## Why the Format Works

![Woman yelling at cat meme format analysis](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1495360010541-f48722b34f7d?w=800&h=400&fit=crop&auto=format "Why the woman yelling at cat format is so effective")

The format succeeds because it captures a universal dynamic: **escalating emotion meeting total indifference.**

The woman (left panel) represents anyone making a passionate argument, presenting information, or insisting on something with great energy. The cat (right panel) represents the audience, the target, or the universe itself — responding with complete calm.

This structure is funny because:

1. **The scale mismatch is relatable.** Most of us have been on both sides — the person yelling, and the cat calmly ignoring the yelling.

2. **The cat's expression is perfect.** Smudge isn't aggressive, confused, or scared. The cat is serenely indifferent, which reads as even more dismissive than outright rejection.

3. **It maps onto almost anything.** Left panel = anyone who cares intensely. Right panel = anything that doesn't respond to that caring. The template generalizes to thousands of situations.

## The Cat's Full Name

For the record: the cat is **Smudge the Cat**, also known as **Smudge Lord** on social media. Smudge is from Ottawa, Canada. The owner regularly posted photos of Smudge, but the dinner table photo became something else entirely.

As of the last update, Smudge passed away in 2020. The meme lives on.
